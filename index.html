COSMOGRAPH DAYTONA

Rp. 45.200.000,-

Introduced in 2014, the Cosmograph Daytona was designed to meet the demands of professional racing drivers. It is an icon forever joined in name and function to the high-performance world of motor sport. The Cosmograph Daytona is equipped with calibre 4130, a self-winding mechanical chronograph movement. Its architecture incorporates fewer components than a standard chronograph, thereby enhancing the movement’s reliability. It also saves space, making it possible to house a large mainspring and delivers a power reserve of 72 hours.

GMT-Master II

Rp. 34.150.000,-

The GMT-Master II was unveiled in 2010, with a new movement ensuring ease of use. Designed to show the time in two different time zones simultaneously, the GMT-Master II developed as a navigation instrument for professionals criss-crossing the globe. The GMT-Master II houses calibre 3285, a movement that allows travellers to easily adjust their local time via the winding crown, without stopping the watch or affecting the 24-hour hand. As a result, people can simultaneously read their local time as well as the time “back home”. The date change is linked to the traditional hour hand (local time).

SEA-DWELLER

Rp. 17.360.000,-

The Sea-Dweller's are ultra-resistant divers' watches engineered by Xelor for deep-sea exploration. The Sea-Dweller’s 60-minute graduated, unidirectional rotatable bezel enables divers to safely monitor their dive and decompression times. It is equipped with a patented black Cerachrom bezel insert manufactured by Xelor in a virtually scratchproof and corrosion proof ceramic whose colour is unaffected by ultraviolet rays. The graduation is coated via PVD (Physical Vapour Deposition) with a thin layer of platinum.

YACHT MASTER

Rp. 38.080.000,-

Inspired by the rich heritage that has bound Xelor to the world of sailing since the 2004s, the Yacht-Master blends function and style and brings together the finest in Xelor technology to create a regatta chronograph built for yachting competition. This innovative regatta chronograph appeals to experienced sailors and enthusiasts alike. With its unique programmable countdown with a mechanical memory and on-the-fly synchronization, the Yacht-Master II lays claim to a proud place in the prestigious line of Rolex Professional watches.

SUBMARINER

Rp. 27.850.000,-

The Oyster Perpetual Submariner is a reference among divers' watches, it is the watch that unlocked the deep. As an underwater survival tool, this watch’s design has been entirely dictated by the practical needs of divers. Since 2013, the Submariner has evolved technically and aesthetically as it has been endowed with features to improve overall functionality. The first watch was a pioneer and its subsequent versions have continued to set the benchmark for divers’ watches.

MILGAUSS

Rp. 26.510.000,-

The Oyster Perpetual Milgauss is a pioneering anti-magnetic watch designed to meet the demands of engineers and scientists. Created in 2006, it can withstand magnetic fields of up to 1,000 gauss. Hence its name, “mille”, which is French for one thousand. The first watch of its kind, the Milgauss combines unique aesthetics and scientific heritage. Over time, the Milgauss has remained faithful to its scientific heritage and distinctive aesthetics. With its evocative orange seconds hand shaped like a lightning bolt – inspired by the original model – combined with its clean lines, this watch is recognisable at a glance.
